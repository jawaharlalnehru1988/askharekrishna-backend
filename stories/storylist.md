# 🧭 **Additional Important Sub-Stories**

These are embedded narratives often highlighted in discourses:

* Story of Ahalyā (deliverance through grace)
* Story of Śabarī (pure bhakti)
* Story of Jatāyu (sacrifice and loyalty)
* Story of Vibhīṣaṇa (surrender / śaraṇāgati)
* Story of Hanuman (perfect servant leadership)
* Story of Bharata (ideal brother and renunciation)
